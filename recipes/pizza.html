Description

Use this recipe to make delicious pizza which you can customize however you want!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can tomato sauce or pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • toppings

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast by mixing sugar and yeast with lukewarm water in a bowl. Let sit until bubbles form on the surface.
  2. Combine dry ingredients and pour in olive oil and yeast mixture and mix until a workable dough is formed
  3. knead dough with hands until it turns soft and fluffy
  4. cover dough with a cloth or plastic wrap and let rise for 4 hours
  5. Preheat oven to to 475 degree Farenheit
  6. Shape and roll dough into a circle and place on a pizza pan
  7. spread tomato or pizza sauce onto the dough
  8. sprinkle shredded cheese over pizza dough evenly until covered with cheese
  9. add in toppings of choice and bake until crust is golden brown
