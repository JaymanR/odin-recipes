Banana Bread

Image by Freepik

Description

This banana bread recipe is easy to follow and you can adjust the ingredients as you need. You can make the dough without a mixer, all you need is a bowl and a spatula. If you want, you can also add chopped walnuts, almonds or blueberry for flavor. This recipe uses baking soda as the dough risener to make nice moist banana bread!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 over ripe bananas
  • 8 tbs melted butter
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 large egg

note: you may add optional ingredients like chopped nuts or reduce the sugar amount to your liking.

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degree feirenheit
  2. mix in dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, salt, sugar together in a large bowl
  3. mash bananas and add in egg and melted butter, stir until mixed well
  4. pour in wet ingrediants to dry ingredients and stir until mixed
  5. add any optional ingrediants to batter and mix well
  6. pour batter into a bread pan and bake for 50-60 mins pr until a toothpick poked at the center comes out clean
