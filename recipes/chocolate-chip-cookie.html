Chocolate Chip Cookies

Image by Freepik

Description

Make some declicious chewy chocolate chip cookies by using these simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbps butter, softened
  • 1.5 cups brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 2.5 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

note: you may add optional ingredients like chopped nuts or reduce the sugar amount to your liking.

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degree feirenheit
  2. mix in dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, salt, sugar together in a large bowl
  3. in a seperate bowl, beat eggs and softened butter and stir until mixed well
  4. pour in wet ingrediants to dry ingredients and stir until dough forms
  5. add any optional ingrediants and chocolate chips to dough and mix well
  6. make 1 inch diameter balls from dough and drop on a cookie baking sheet
  7. bake untill cookies are golden brown
